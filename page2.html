CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
PROTAGONISTS
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin [Ibarra]
  • Ibarra is a young ilustrado who has just come back home to the Philippines after seven years of studing in Europe.
  • He is tall, brown(kayumanggi), and you can see the honesty in his face.
Maria Clara de los Santos [Maria Clara]
  • Maria Clara is a beautiful young lady in the higher class of society who has spent the last seven years in a convent.
  • She is the daughter of Kapitan Tiago and the goddaughter of Padre Damaso.
ANTAGONIST
Padre Damaso
  • Padre Damaso is a friar that is the godfather of Maria Clara.
  • He was kicked from San Diego because of tasking someone to dig for a body and switch its cemetery.
SIDE CHARACTERS
Santiago de los Santos [Kapitan Tiago]
  • Kapitan Tiago is a wealthy and famous man in the area of Binondo, or Binundok.
  • His wife is Donya Pia Alba and his daughter is said to be Maria Clara.
  • He was very excited in the arrival of Ibarra in the Philippines.
Sisa
  • She is the mother of Crispin and Basilio; she is the wife of Pedro.
  • Sisa lives a poor life with her family.
Basilio
  • He is a poor boy who is the brother of Crispin.
  • Basilio dreams of giving a good life to his brother and his mother, Sisa.
Crispin
  • He is a poor boy who is the brother of Basilio.
  • Basilio was accused of stealing and eventually sent to be punished by the guards and priest.
  • After the tragedy of his capturing, he was never found in the story again.
